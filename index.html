    Privacy Policy

    This app collects your email, profile picture and user Id from a secure Firebase connection and transmits your GPS position for the purpose of My Fitness installation. It will continue to run in the background until you stop the workout or challenge. Your GPS position is encrypted and stored in our database with a unique id, but no other identifying information. Your data will not be resold or distributed to any other third parties.
